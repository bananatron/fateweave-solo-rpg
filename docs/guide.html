Fateweaver’s Quest

Solo Adventure Rules Companion

Why this page?

Quick reference for how to use the helper app and how to keep the story moving forward.

1 • Start a Game

  1. Name your hero (or click “Random Name”).
  2. Pick an Archetype and a Talent.
  3. Press “Begin Adventure” – the log records your opening scene.

2 • Screen Guide

  • Character panel – name, archetype, talent, goal, scars.
  • Story Clock – fill a segment when tension rises (see §4).
  • Story Log – chronicle every outcome. “Undo” is for typos only; in Fateweaver’s Quest the fiction never rewinds.
  • Tabs – Map, Actions, Oracle, Details, NPC, Monsters, Factions, Quests, Journal.

3 • Dice & Consequences

Fate Dice (2d6)

  • Any 6 ⇒ Great Success
  • Total 8‑11 ⇒ Success + Complication
  • Total 4‑7 ⇒ Failure + Opportunity
  • Total <4 ⇒ Disaster

Combat Roll

Click “Combat Roll”; the app explains scars and enemy results.

Scars

Mark one when fiction demands it. Three scars = hero falls.

Story Clock (6 segments)

  • Fill one on every Failure, Disaster, or dramatic time jump.
  • At six, a Major Event triggers, then the clock resets.

4 • Scene Loop

  1. Frame – where are you and what do you want?
  2. Act – move, talk, fight, ask the Oracle, or trigger a generator.
  3. Roll when success is uncertain.
  4. Record the outcome in the Story Log (no deletions except errors).
  5. Advance when the immediate goal is resolved, the location changes, or time clearly passes.

5 • Extended Example of Play

Setup
-----
Lyra the Mage (Talent: Keen Eye)
Goal: Find a cure for the plague haunting her homeland.

Story Log
---------
Lyra the Mage begins her quest: Find a cure for the plague haunting her homeland.

New Room
--------
You discover a new room: Empty
Detail Check: Glowing Crystals (Ceremonial) for Knowledge - feeling Curious, currently Investigating
Lyra notes arcane sigils carved into the crystal‑lit chamber.

NPC Encounter
-------------
Met Tessa the Scholar - Hooded, Curious, wants Information
Oracle: "Does Tessa know anything about a cure?" – Yes, but...
Tessa recalls an ancient tonic recipe but needs a missing ingredient guarded by the Iron Crown.

Quest Hook
----------
Quest Generated: Retrieve Quest
Related to: The Iron Crown
Retrieve an artifact important to The Iron Crown
Lyra vows to sneak into the Iron Crown’s vaults for the ingredient.

Travel & Clock
--------------
Lyra hikes across storm‑lashed moors (1 Story Clock segment filled).

Infiltration
------------
You discover a new room: Treasure
Detail Check: Whispering Chains (Locked) for Containment - feeling Suspicious, currently Guarding
A jeweled coffer sits behind iron bars; a lone sentry slumbers nearby.

Oracle & Talent
---------------
Lyra uses Keen Eye: extra question while exploring.
Oracle: "Is the coffer trapped?" – No
Lyra silently lifts the lid, seizing the vial of silver dust.

Alarm & Combat
--------------
You discover a new room: Monster
Encountered: Swift Undead that speaks in riddles
Combat Roll: 6 + 4 = 10 - Success with complication
Trade blows - enemy wounded, you take a Scar
Lyra’s warding spell shatters the undead husk, but a talon gashes her arm (1 Scar).

Major Event Trigger
-------------------
Story Clock now at 6: MAJOR EVENT: A faction makes a major move!
The Iron Crown mobilizes troops, searching for the thief of their sacred vial.

Aftermath
---------
Lyra escapes into the night, clutching the cure’s final ingredient.

6 • Ending the Tale

Achieve the goal, suffer three scars, or choose to close the book—then write one last Story Log line to seal your legend.
